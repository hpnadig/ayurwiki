# Erythroxylum monogynum - Red Cedar, Natkadeodar, Sembulichan, Gatiri, Devataru

[TOC]

![](../images/(Erythroxylum_monogynum)_Red_cedar_foliage_at_Kambalakonda_01.jpg)

**Erythroxylum monogynum** is valued for its wood, which is used locally. It also has medicinal applications, yields an essential oil and a wood oil. This plant is belongs to Erythroxylaceae family.
## Uses
Fever, Stomachache, Skin deseases, Joint pain, Kidney problems, Urogenital diseases, Urinary tract problems, Rheumatic conditions, Itching, Skin rashes.

## Parts Used
Fruits, Leaves.

## Chemical Composition
Phytochemical constituents of leaf of Erythroxylum monogynum were studied and
it was found that it contains alkaloids, carbohydrates, flavinoids and glycosides in all AqE,
AcE and CD.

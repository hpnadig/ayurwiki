# Tulsi

Tulsi, or Holy Basil, is considered sacred in Hinduism and revered for its medicinal properties. It is an adaptogenic herb that helps balance the mind and body.

## Botanical Description

Ocimum tenuiflorum is an aromatic perennial plant in the Lamiaceae family, growing to 30-60 cm with hairy stems and fragrant leaves.

## Medicinal Properties

Tulsi is known for its immunomodulatory, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties. It is considered a natural immunity booster and stress reliever.

## Traditional Uses

- Respiratory infections
- Cough and cold
- Stress and anxiety
- Fever
- Digestive disorders

## Dosage

Leaf juice: 10-20 ml daily
Powder: 2-4 grams
Tea: 1-2 cups daily

# Ashwagandha

Ashwagandha is one of the most revered medicinal herbs in Ayurvedic tradition. Known as the "Indian Ginseng," it is classified as a Rasayana (rejuvenating) herb that promotes vitality and longevity.

## Botanical Description

Withania somnifera is a compact, evergreen shrub belonging to the Solanaceae family. It grows to a height of 1-2 meters with small greenish-yellow flowers and red berry-like fruits.

## Medicinal Properties

Ashwagandha is renowned for its adaptogenic properties, helping the body cope with physical and mental stress. It is considered Balya (strength-promoting) and Rasayana (rejuvenating).

## Traditional Uses

- General debility and fatigue
- Nervous exhaustion
- Insomnia and anxiety
- Rheumatism and joint pain
- Respiratory conditions

## Dosage

Root powder: 3-6 grams daily with warm milk

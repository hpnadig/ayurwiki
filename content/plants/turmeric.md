# Turmeric

Turmeric is a golden-hued spice that has been used for millennia in Ayurveda and Indian cuisine. It is known for its powerful anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

## Botanical Description

Curcuma longa is a perennial plant of the Zingiberaceae family, growing to about 1 meter in height with oblong leaves and cylindrical rhizomes.

## Medicinal Properties

Turmeric contains curcumin, a compound with potent anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties. It is considered a natural purifier and healer.

## Traditional Uses

- Inflammation and joint disorders
- Digestive issues
- Skin conditions
- Liver detoxification
- Wound healing

## Dosage

Powder: 1-3 grams daily with milk or warm water
Fresh rhizome: 1-2 grams
